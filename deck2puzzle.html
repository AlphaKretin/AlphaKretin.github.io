Test Hand Puzzle Generator

Upload a deck with the file dialog or by dragging and dropping the file onto the dashed region. (YGOPercy folder/deck

After you have upload your .ydk (Deck file) using the button above select the board you'd like to face it'll download a .lua file. Simply go back to your YGOPro Percy folder and go to Single and paste it in there then open up Percy and go to puzzle mode and look for the file (Hands_) than your deck name and click the Moprhein card to draw a starting hand. you can also control the AI to simulate "Correct" playing
Please note the following:
• If it crashes straight after loading just re open the program and give it a few seconds
• You can't by default simulate going second (However you can attack on the first turn) so after drawing a 5 card hand from "Morinphen" go to your end phase than it'll restart your turn and you draw a card for turn (Also applies if you want to gain your normal summon again
• You cannot "reset" the cards in the AI's hand. just simply hit leave and re-open the puzzle.
